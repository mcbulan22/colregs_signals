🔔 rapid ring ~5 s
At intervals of not more than 1 minute
Vessel ≥100 m

Rule 35(h) — Vessel aground

3 strokes + 🔔 rapid ~5 s + 3 strokes
Three separate bell strokes before and after the rapid bell ring. Plus (g) gong signal if applicable. May also sound an appropriate whistle signal

< 12 m
